## 14. INTERACTIVE ELEMENTS
- **Smooth Scroll:** Navigation links scroll smoothly
- **Hover Effects:** Cards lift/shadow on hover

## 15. PERFORMANCE & ACCESSIBILITY
- **Lighthouse Score Target:** 90+ on Performance & Accessibility
- **Image Optimization:** Use WebP with fallbacks
- **Alt Text:** All images must have descriptive alt text
- **WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance:** High contrast, keyboard navigation
- **Meta Tags:** SEO-optimized descriptions and keywords

## 17. ADMISSIONS STRATEGY (Best UX Practice)
**Primary CTA Approach:**
- **"Get More Information"** button on Hero Section ‚Üí Scrolls to Contact Section
- Contact Section includes:
  - Inquiry Form (Name, Email, Phone, Grade Level, Message)
  - Direct phone numbers (Admissions hotline)
  - Email contact
  - Location & hours
  - Google Map embed
- **Rationale:** Captures parent interest immediately without redirecting to incomplete pages
- **Benefit:** Direct communication channel, collects inquiries, builds database
